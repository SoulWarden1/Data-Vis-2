Coffee Culture in Melbourne

Melbourne is renowned for its vibrant coffee culture, with numerous cafés scattered throughout the city. We will now explore the intricacies of Melbourne's coffee scene.

Cafés in Melbourne

Top 10 Suburbs by Cafés per Capita

Many of Melbourne's cafes are located in the inner-city suburbs. The further out from the city center, the fewer cafés there are. There are some outliers in the data caused by extremely low population density.

Cafés in Melbourne Over Time

Melbourne's coffee culture has seen significant growth over the years, with a steady increase in the number of cafés. This trend reflects the city's growing appreciation for quality coffee and the vibrant café scene that has become an integral part of Melbourne's identity. This growth has been impacted by COVID and the rise of working from home, but there is a steady rate of recovery.
