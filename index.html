Coffee Culture in Melbourne

Melbourne is renowned for its vibrant café culture, with thousands of cafés spread across the metropolitan area. This visualisation explores café density, travel patterns, pricing, and how Melbourne compares with the rest of the world.

Cafés Over Time

Melbourne’s café scene has steadily expanded over time, reflecting the city’s strong coffee culture. Growth slowed during COVID-19, though recent years show recovery and continued expansion.
